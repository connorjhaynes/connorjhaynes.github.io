In the Summer of 2025 I attended the LSU RTG program, where I was a part of the Hyperplane Arrangements group. Over a period of two weeks we worked through the first three lectures of Stanley's An Introduction to Hyperplane Arrangements. We also presented a final expository talk centered around computing the chromatic polynomial of a graph via the characteristic polynomial of the corresponding graphic arrangement.

I gave three talks during this period, the notes for which can be found here .
